# Sample Constraint Brief (format demonstration)

This is ONE filled-in brief so you can see how the 5 categories work. It is NOT
a template for any industry. Your project fills in its OWN reality, which may be
the exact opposite of what's here. The skill presets nothing; it only enforces
what YOU put in.

(Shown with a car rental that forbids the word "van" and has no counter. A
different car rental might rent vans and promote its counter; in that case the
brief would say the opposite, and the skill would follow that instead. Same
framework, different contents.)

PROJECT: A car rental whose fleet is minibuses + MPVs, with no airport counter.

1. DO-NOT-MENTION
   - No in-terminal desks or airport counters (this business has none).
   - No young-driver surcharges.

2. FACT-CORRECTIONS
   - Fleet is minibuses and MPVs, NOT cargo vans. Never call them vans.
   - Shuttle service exists at one location only, via booking confirmation.
     Do not generalize it to other locations.

3. COMPLIANCE-REDLINES
   - [project-specific legal lines, if any; leave empty if none]

4. VOICE
   - Each location page keeps its own established voice. Match the reference;
     do not flatten every page into one house tone.

5. TERMINOLOGY
   - Use "minibus" / "MPV". Never "van".

---
How to use this: ignore the contents, copy the 5-category SHAPE, fill each with
your project's truth. Any category can be empty. The skill will also walk you
through building this if you don't have one ready.
